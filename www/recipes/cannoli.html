Cannoli

READY IN: 1hr 5mins          SERVES: 4

Ingredients

150g plain flour
1 tbsp golden caster sugar

For the filling
250g ricotta
, drained and beaten until fluffy
100g mascarpone

You will also need
cannoli moulds

Directions:

Tip the flour, sugar, bicarb, cinnamon and cocoa (if using) into a bowl with a pinch of salt.
Fill a deep-fat fryer, wok or deep saucepan a third of the way up with oil.
Deep-fry the cannoli (with their moulds) one at a time, making sure they cook all over
Serve soon after filling.
