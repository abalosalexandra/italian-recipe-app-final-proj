Crispy roast pork shoulder (porchetta)

READY IN: 1hr 5mins          SERVES: 4

Ingredients

2.5kg pork shoulder, skin on
1 teaspoon caraway seeds
1 tablespoon fennel seeds
1/2 cup coarsely chopped oregano
1/4 cup coarsely chopped flat-leaf parsley
2 tablespoons coarsely chopped rosemary
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons sea salt flakes
1/2 cup (125ml) dry white wine
1/2 cup (125ml) Massel chicken style liquid stock

Directions:

Preheat oven to 130C. Place pork on a clean work surface. Use a sharp knife to score the rind in 1cm intervals
Place caraway and half the fennel seeds in a small frying pan over low heat. Cook, tossing, for 1 minute until aromatic.
Increase heat to 240C. Pour the combined wine and stock around the pork
